~Inductions 2023~

So hear me all and listen good, your effort will be worth the gold. If you will be wise and solve the mess, the ultimate destination is a ticket to LDS, NIT Jamshedpur.

1. Learn from the legend of musk deer, don’t run around finding for the solutions, it might (or rather will) be in front of you.
2. Take a screenshot of this page, it will act as a map in your journey.
3. Easiest place to hide is plain sight.
4. Look for the Underlined words, they’ll lead you ahead in this journey of decoding the Enigma.
5. Remember we are a literary society in an engineering college.
6. Whenever you stumble upon a numerical quantity, take two steps beyond the dot. This is precisely what you need.
7. Whenever you write something remember that we hate Mr. Upper Case and Mrs. Space. They always trouble us with their relationship problems.
8. Nobody on this planet but your parents have set the final piece of the puzzle.
9. Mr. Google Account is a friend of ours. He only tells what you want us to tell. If you use something that we don’t know you’ll be in trouble.
10. Google has helped us to dig this pit for you, so take its help to climb up!
11. Finally, we prefer NIT over National Institute of Technology.
12. For starters whatever you see is the password.

Best of Luck Comrades! Waiting for you on the other side, only if you make it!
